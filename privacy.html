Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

We do not collect, store, or share your resume bullet points. Text you enter is sent directly to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate rewrites and is not stored on our servers.

Payment information

Payments are processed securely by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your credit card information.

Cookies

We use localStorage (a browser-based storage) only to count your free rewrites. No tracking cookies, no analytics, no advertising.

Third parties

We use Anthropic's API to process rewrites and Lemon Squeezy for payments. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.
